## Your First Quick Log

Try it now:

1. Press **Ctrl+Y Ctrl+Y** (Mac: Cmd+Y Cmd+Y)
2. Pick an issue
3. Enter hours: `2h`, `30m`, or `1.5`
4. Done.

No browser. No page loads. Just logged.

### Time Formats

All of these work:
- `2` or `2h` → 2 hours
- `30m` → 30 minutes
- `1:30` or `1h 30m` → 1.5 hours
