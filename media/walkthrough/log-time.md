## Log Your Time

### Quick Log (Ctrl+Y Ctrl+Y)

The fastest way to log time:

1. Press `Ctrl+Y Ctrl+Y` (Mac: `Cmd+Y Cmd+Y`)
2. Pick issue → Enter hours → Done

Formats: `2h`, `30m`, `1.5`, `1:30`, `1h 30min`

### Structured Work Sessions

Want focused work with breaks? Use the **Timer**:

1. Press `Ctrl+Y Ctrl+T` to plan your day
2. Create work units (45min work + 15min break)
3. Timer auto-logs time when each unit completes

### Staging Tasks (Optional)

Use **Kanban** view to stage tasks before timing them:
- Add notes and priorities
- Click "Add to Today's Plan" when ready
